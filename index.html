The Challenge

An Energy Divide Is Coming

As the global energy transition accelerates, a new divide is emerging—one that risks leaving farming communities and rural populations behind, much like the digital divide.

Our Answer: Regenerative Govar-based Climate Solutions

Anaadi Ecofutures works to harness the untapped potential of Govar (cow dung) through scalable, sustainable, decentralized climate solutions.

Operating at the intersection of research, design, and deployment, we build integrated systems that convert farm waste into clean energy, sustainable materials, and bio-based inputs—creating continuous revenue streams for farmers.

🔬

Research

Advancing govar-based biofuels, bio-construction materials, and sustainable packaging.

🤝

Design

Translating research into deployable models by working with farms, institutions, and communities to design scalable, decentralized systems.

🌾

Deployment

Bringing solutions to market through products and on-ground systems, from bio-materials to chemical-free agriculture, demonstrating viable, scalable outcomes built from Govar.

The Advantage

Why Govar?

Sustainable

Govar is a renewable, carbon-circular resource that converts agricultural waste into clean energy while reducing methane emissions.

Resilient

Locally available and abundant, govar reduces dependence on volatile fossil fuel supply chains and strengthens rural energy security.

Decentralized

Govar-based systems enable distributed energy production at the farm level, lowering infrastructure costs and improving access in rural areas.

Stronger ESG Scores

Compared to coal and oil, govar-based energy improves ESG performance by lowering carbon intensity, enhancing waste utilization, and generating measurable social impact through farmer income.

Our Journey

From Roots to Future

A decade of turning tradition into innovation — every milestone rooted in purpose.

2015

Bio Plant Established

Set up our first biogas plant — proving that cow dung could be a viable, scalable source of clean energy for rural communities.

2017

CREST 2017 — Cow-Based Sustainability Conference

Participated in the CREST conference, presenting research on cow-based sustainability practices and connecting with a wider network of like-minded researchers and practitioners.

2018

Capacity Building for Locals

Launched hands-on training and workshops for local farmers and communities — transferring knowledge on organic farming, govar utilisation, and sustainable living practices.

2019

Govar in Action: Making of the Kutir

Documented the construction of our kutir (cottage) built entirely using govar-based bio-construction materials — a living proof of concept. Read the blog →

2020

Chula & Boiler Systems

Designed and deployed efficient chula (cookstove) and boiler systems powered by govar — reducing smoke, saving fuel, and improving daily life for rural households. Read the blog →

2024

Anaadi Ecofutures — Officially Established

Formalised years of research and ground-level work into Anaadi Ecofutures — a company dedicated to building scalable, decentralised climate solutions from govar.

The Road Ahead

Scaling the Vision

Bio-construction at scale, govar-based biofuels for aviation, nationwide capacity-building workshops, and partnerships that take sustainable energy from the farm to the world.
